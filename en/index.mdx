Notion Rhino builds premium Notion templates for people who want to spend less time setting things up and more time actually getting things done. Whether you're a freelancer managing clients, a founder tracking projects, or a student organizing your life — our templates give you a running start.

Every template we ship is:

- **Thoughtfully designed** — clean, minimal, and built with Notion best practices in mind
- **Ready to use** — duplicate and go, no configuration required
- **Documented** — each template comes with a guide so you know exactly how to get the most out of it
