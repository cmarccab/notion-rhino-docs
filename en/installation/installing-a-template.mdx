## Installation Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your template">
    After purchase, open the template link you received. This takes you to a shared Notion page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Duplicate">
    In the top-right corner of the page, click the **Duplicate** button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your workspace">
    Select the Notion workspace where you want to install the template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using it">
    The template is now in your workspace. Customize it however you like — rename things, adjust views, add your data.
  </Step>
</Steps>
