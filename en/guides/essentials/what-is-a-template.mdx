title: What is a template?
description: Learn what a Notion template is and how duplicating one gives you a fully built workspace in seconds.
---

A Notion template is a pre-built page or workspace you can duplicate into your own Notion account. Think of it as a ready-made system — the structure, databases, views, and logic are already set up. You just add your own content.

## How it works

When you purchase a template from Notion Rhino, you get a link to a shared Notion page. From there, it's one click to duplicate it into your workspace.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get the template link">
    After purchase, you'll receive a link to the template page in Notion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Duplicate">
    Open the link and click the **Duplicate** button in the top-right corner of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your workspace">
    Select the Notion workspace you want to add the template to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using it">
    The template is now yours. Customize it however you like — rename things, adjust views, add your data.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What comes with a Notion Rhino template?

Every template we ship includes:

- **Pre-configured databases** — tables, boards, calendars, and timelines are already built and linked
- **Multiple views** — see your data the way that works best for you
- **Documentation** — each template comes with a guide explaining how it works and how to get the most out of it

<Info>
  Duplicating a template creates a completely independent copy in your workspace. Changes you make don't affect the original, and changes we make to the template don't affect your copy.
</Info>
