Notion is a flexible productivity app that combines notes, documents, databases, and project management into a single workspace. Instead of juggling separate apps for tasks, wikis, and spreadsheets, Notion lets you build exactly the system you need — all in one place.

## What can you do with Notion?

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Write & document" icon="pen-line">
    Create notes, wikis, SOPs, meeting notes, and long-form documents with a clean, distraction-free editor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manage projects" icon="kanban">
    Track tasks and projects with boards, timelines, calendars, and table views — all driven by the same database.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Build a personal system" icon="layout-dashboard">
    Design a workspace that fits the way you think — habit trackers, goal planners, dashboards, and more.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Work with a team" icon="users">
    Share pages, collaborate in real time, and keep your team aligned with shared wikis and project spaces.
  </Card>
</Columns>

## Why people love it

Notion is powerful because it's a blank canvas. You're not locked into one way of doing things. The same tool that runs a startup's entire operations can also work as a student's study planner.

The downside of that flexibility is that setting up a good system takes time. That's where Notion Rhino comes in — our templates give you a solid starting point so you can skip the setup and start getting things done.
